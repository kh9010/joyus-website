## Starting Point (already decided)

Kahran's framing: **"We think with everyone. We build with a few."** Advisory is the primary offering and the front door. Operational/production work is the premium tier -- selective, not default. The site copy has been updated to reflect this directionally. Use pass 1 to pressure-test this with Divya and refine it.

---

## The Big Questions

Answer these together. Write messy, argue, circle back. This isn't a deliverable — it's a conversation that becomes your guide for passes 2-3.

### 1. Who are we talking to?

- Who's our ideal client? (Be specific — not "startups" but "early-stage founders who need to look credible before they feel credible")
- Who's our ideal reader/listener? (Might be different from clients)
- Who are we NOT for?

### 2. What's our one sentence?

- If someone lands on the site and leaves after 10 seconds, what do they take away?
- Current vibe check: read the homepage, about page, and services page out loud. Does it sound like one voice or three different pitches?

### 3. What makes us *us*?

- What do Kahran and Divya each bring that you can't get from a bigger agency or a solo freelancer?
- Where does the podcast/comics/writing work connect to the client work? Is that connection clear on the site?
- Are we leaning into the weirdness enough? Too much?
