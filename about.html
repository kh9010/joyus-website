About Joyus

Joyus is an Experience Design Studio. We look at life as being made up of experiences, and we believe that everything in this human experience can be designed to be more — more filled with delight, more filled with joy, more filled with a sense of connection and belonging.

We seek out people who share our belief in the power of problem solving by finding new models, new frames of reference, and new ways of thinking.

Through our work, we deliver experiences that wow your customers today, help them remember you tomorrow and keep them connected to you forever. After all, joy and delight is a non-zero-sum game.

Our Founders

Kahran Singh

Co-founder, Strategy

Kahran brings product design, product marketing, and strategic storytelling to Joyus. With over a decade of experience across education, travel, technology and consumer goods — working across the US, UK, EU, and India — he's built a unique perspective on problem-solving and an ability to adapt to wildly different markets and client needs.

As an active angel investor with deep connections to both the Seattle and Indian startup ecosystems, Kahran works closely with CEOs and founders to find clarity in their messaging and build lasting relationships with the people who matter most to their business. He holds a B.Sc. in Computer Science from Tufts University.

Divya Tak

Co-founder, Design

Divya is an artist at heart, storyteller in the head, with designer hands. Her background spans experience design, product design & development, and game design. She uses the newest technology and insights into consumer behavior to craft culture-shaping experiences across industries including technology, consumer goods, and education.

Divya has a Master's degree in Physics from IIT Kharagpur and taught herself design by making a lot of things. Her illustrations — playful characters, intricate watercolors, and expressive line work — have become a signature of Joyus's creative output, from the Tatsam mental health app to Secret Senses packaging to The Line Before, a poetry installation exhibited at Boulder Public Library.
