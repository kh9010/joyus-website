About Joyus

We work on projects with impact. The projects it feels impossible to hire consultants for. The projects that change the lives of the people who touch them.

Our stories are fierce. Businesses built on dreams. Sandcastles carved from the sky and made real on the earth.

Joyus is a design and strategy studio founded by Divya Tak and Kahran Singh. We bring a holistic, playful approach to projects that span product design, brand building, game development, system design, and storytelling. We believe the projects that'll haunt you until the day they're made are the ones worth doing.

Our Founders

Divya Tak

Co-founder, Design

Divya is a designer and illustrator whose work spans visual identity, product design, and hand-drawn illustration. Her illustrations — playful characters, intricate watercolors, and expressive line work — have become a signature of Joyus's creative output. From the Tatsam mental health app to Secret Senses packaging, Divya brings warmth and craft to every project.

Kahran Singh

Co-founder, Strategy

Kahran brings a blend of strategic thinking, product design, and storytelling to Joyus. With experience spanning startups, nonprofits, and established companies, he specializes in helping teams find the narrative that moves people — whether that's investors, donors, or end users. His work with Klydo, TomboyX, and PrathamUSA demonstrates a commitment to understanding people before designing solutions.
