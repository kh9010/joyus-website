How do different understandings of 'what you do' affect the course of a company? How do they affect you as a person? Kahran and Divya talk about how they got to joyus studio's single line vision, and share ideas to help others get there with their learnings.

notable moments

One of the hardest things is describing what you do when what you do doesn't fit neatly into a category. The difficulty of the description is actually the strength of the work.

You can make things lower stakes for yourself so that it's easier and you can try more things. Lower stakes doesn't mean less important — it means less pressure to be perfect.
