Thinking on Courage vs Daring

April 05, 202326 min ideas

listen elsewhere Spotify → Apple Podcasts →

We always applaud courage but not always daring? Daring is for rogues and courage is for knights, whether they choose to be good or evil. Do we always want to foster courage, but not always daring?

Divya and Kahran explore this topic, and share stories, tidbits and a sprinkle of joy of life in-between.
