What we do, when you ask

DiagnoseResearchShapeEvolve

Six things we do — built around a four-step way of thinking through every project.

01 — of 06

advise

Most of what we do is thinking with you, not for you.

Meaning: a room, a shared problem, and questions we ask until the shape of the answer is clear. Sometimes the recommendation is to do nothing. Sometimes it is to start over. Sometimes a deck comes out of it. But it always starts here, in the room, with the thinking.

  • Strategic sounding-board retainersongoing
  • Investor-facing narrative work2–4 wks
  • Product & audience diagnostics2 wks
  • Founder coachingrecurring
02 — of 06

design

When thinking has produced a decision, we make the artifacts that carry it.

Decks, identity systems, interfaces, packaging, editorial. Whatever gives the decision a shape people can see and work with. Design here is translation, not decoration — the visible argument for what the team has already chosen to believe.

  • Brand identity & visual systems3–6 wks
  • Product & interface design6–12 wks
  • Illustration & packagingvaries
  • Editorial & narrative design1–4 wks
03 — of 06

build

Selective. When we own delivery, we own the whole shape — no handoff.

  • Zero-to-one product launches8–16 wks
  • GTM sprints with a real deadline6–10 wks
  • Full brand builds under pressure4–8 wks
  • Research → prototype → ship10–14 wks

We take on a handful of build engagements a year, end to end. Because the worst thing for a client is a studio that leaves halfway through. The second-worst is one that never should have started.

04 — of 06

manage

Two of us in your channel, every day, for as long as the project needs.

  • Embedded design leadershipquarter
  • Fractional head-of-productquarter+
  • Research-ops ownershipproject
  • Hiring your first brand/design lead6–10 wks

It is literal. You are not handed off to a junior, or to an account manager, or to a team that needs to be briefed all over again. The people who scope the work are the people making it — and they are in your channel.

05 — of 06

tell

The story that unifies the team is usually the same story that lands with investors and customers.

  • Pitch decks & investor narratives2–4 wks
  • Website copy & positioning3–6 wks
  • Founder origin stories2 wks
  • Internal narrative docs1–3 wks

When a company struggles to explain itself, the story is rarely the problem — the telling is. We spend time with the people who know the business best and find the version of the story that lands the same way for investors, customers, new hires, and each other.

06 — of 06

grow

After launch, the work isn't done. We stay long enough to see what worked and what didn't.

Launch day is where most engagements end. We think it's where the real work starts — the tuning, the listening, the small calibrations that separate a product people forget from one they come back to.

  • Post-launch tuning retainersmonthly
  • Behavioral & engagement designproject
  • Creator & community strategyproject
  • Growth vs engagement frameworks2–4 wks
