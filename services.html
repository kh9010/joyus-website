Working together

The best work happens when you bring us in early

We're a two-person studio. We don't hand off decks and disappear. We sit with you, figure out what's actually going on, and build the thing alongside you. Whether that's one conversation or a year-long partnership, we adapt to where you are.

Brand Strategy & Storytelling

Every company has a story. The problem is almost never the story itself — it's how it's being told. We help founding teams find the narrative spine of their business: the thing that makes investors lean in, customers stay, and employees actually care. This might mean reworking a pitch deck, building an investor narrative from scratch, or just sitting with you until the argument clicks.

We've done this across fundraising rounds, board presentations, and go-to-market launches. The work is always specific to you — your stage, your market, your voice. We're not writing copy. We're finding the core of what you're trying to say.

Visual Design & Identity

Visual language is not decoration — it's how people feel before they've read a single word. We build brand systems, packaging, and visual identities that carry meaning. Divya's background in illustration and design means everything we make has a handcrafted quality, even when it scales. From watercolor packaging for Secret Senses to the full visual language for Tatsam's mental wellness platform, we design things that feel like they belong to you.

Case Study Secret Senses — hand-drawn watercolors from sketchbook to shelf →

Behavioral Design

We design for how people actually behave — not how a spreadsheet says they should. That means gamification, user journey mapping, and understanding the real decision points where people drop off or lean in. Kahran's background in behavioral economics and Divya's experience in game design come together here: we build systems that respect how humans work, not how we wish they did.

Case Study ConveGenius — gamification for 100K students, designed around real behavior →

Thinking with AI

We use AI tools every day in our own practice — Claude, ChatGPT, Midjourney — and we run workshops for teams that want to get past the hype and build real workflows. The difference between AI as a gimmick and AI as a thinking partner is enormous, and most teams are stuck on the wrong side of that line. We help you cross it.

Coaching & Advisory

Sometimes you don't need a project — you need a thinking partner. We work alongside founders over weeks and months, helping sharpen narratives, prepare for high-stakes moments, and think through decisions that don't have obvious answers. This is not therapy and it's not mentorship. It's two people who have been in the room before, helping you get ready for yours.

How Every Project Works

No matter the format, the rhythm is the same.

01

Diagnose

We figure out what's actually going on — where people drop off, what's not clicking, and where the real opportunity is hiding.

02

Research

We dig into what you already know — your data, your intuitions, your context — and layer on what we know about how people actually make decisions.

03

Build

We make the thing — alongside you, not in a vacuum. The people you're building for stay at the center of every decision.

04

Evolve

We check how things landed, learn from what happened, and look for what to build next. The best projects keep going.

Indicative pricing

A conversation starts at $200. Workshops start at $2,000. Longer partnerships are custom — scoped to your stage, your constraints, and what you actually need. We'd rather talk about fit than quote a number.

Who We Work With

We work best with founders, CEOs, and product leaders who know something needs to change but need help figuring out what — and how to make it land.

  • Startups & SMEs Early-stage teams figuring out their product, their story, or both. People who want a thought partner, not a 90-slide deck.
