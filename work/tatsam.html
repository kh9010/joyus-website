Tatsam

Designing the visual language for mental wellness in India

Client Tatsam
Location India
Category Mental Health App
Services Visual Language, Brand Building, Product Strategy
4 Disciplines working in harmony
India Designing for a stigma-heavy space
End-to-end Visual language to product strategy

The Challenge

Tatsam set out to build a mental health app for India — a market where mental wellness carries real cultural stigma. The challenge wasn't just app design. It was creating an entire visual and emotional language from scratch, one that could make mental health feel approachable, warm, and culturally resonant. Every color, every illustration, every word needed to say: you're safe here.

Western mental health apps were too clinical. Indian equivalents felt dated. There was a genuine white space, but filling it required getting the visual language exactly right — because in this category, design IS the product.

Our Approach

Joyus built the visual system from first principles. The botanical illustration language Divya developed became the emotional core — organic, alive, Indian without being kitsch. We chose colors from the warm end of the spectrum, forms that felt soft and unguarded. The brand identity work went well beyond logos into a complete emotional framework guiding every touchpoint, from onboarding screens to daily check-in flows.

Product strategy ran in parallel with visual language. Features were only added when they served real user needs — we pushed back hard on anything that felt feature-box-ticking. The bespoke solutioning work meant developing custom approaches to genuinely novel problems: how do you prompt someone to open up about anxiety? What does a safe space look like on a 6-inch screen?

The Outcome

Tatsam launched with a visual identity unlike anything in the Indian mental health space. The brand language became a key differentiator, helping users engage with mental wellness content without the stigma barrier that had blocked earlier products. The botanical illustration system became the brand's most recognizable asset — a visual shorthand for warmth, care, and quiet safety.
