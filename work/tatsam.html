an onboarding that doesn't overwhelm

tatsam Visual Language Building Brand Building Product Strategy Bespoke Solutioning

A research-backed onboarding flow — designed in collaboration with psychologists to build intimacy with the client without overwhelming them.

Stage 1 — Initial Rapport

Step 1: sign-up starts with creating a space for feelings & trust to build rapport with new users.

Stage 2 — Life Details

Step 2: gathering details on the user's life and what they might like to change or evolve helps them feel seen and understood.

Stage 3 — Goal Selection

Step 3: giving a sense of agency in deciding on goals aligns incentives and increases motivation.

Stage 4 — Plan Creation

Step 4: create an achievable plan that leverages users' initial enthusiasm to start the behavior change.
