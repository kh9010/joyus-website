Tatsam

Visual Language, Brand Building, Product Strategy, Bespoke Solutioning

Client Tatsam
Location India
Category Mental Health App
Services Visual Language, Brand Building, Product Strategy
4 design disciplines
Mental health designing for India's most sensitive space
End-to-end from visual language to product strategy

Challenge

Tatsam set out to create a mental health app for India — a market where mental wellness carries deep cultural stigma. The challenge wasn't just designing an app; it was creating an entire visual and emotional language that could make mental health feel approachable, warm, and culturally resonant. The product needed to balance clinical trustworthiness with emotional accessibility.

Approach

Joyus brought a holistic approach spanning visual language, brand identity, product strategy, and bespoke solutioning. We developed a visual system that communicated care without clinical coldness, using colors and forms that felt grounded in Indian aesthetic sensibilities. The brand building went beyond logos — we created a complete emotional framework that guided every touchpoint, from onboarding to daily check-ins. Our product strategy work ensured the features served real user needs rather than just ticking feature-list boxes.

Outcome

Tatsam launched with a visual identity that felt genuinely different from Western mental health apps. The brand language became a key differentiator, enabling users to engage with mental wellness content without the stigma barrier that had limited adoption of earlier products in the Indian market.
