Agemo / CodeWords

Making AI-powered development approachable

Client Agemo / CodeWords
Category Developer Tools
Services Research, Product Strategy, Interface Design
Research Deep user discovery
Strategy Product direction from insights
Design Interface that meets users where they are

The Challenge

Agemo was building CodeWords, a tool to bring AI-powered development capabilities to engineers who didn't live at the bleeding edge of AI tooling. The challenge was both product and communication: how do you make a genuinely complex technology feel approachable without dumbing it down? How do you serve developers who are sophisticated but not necessarily AI-native?

Our Approach

We started with research — talking to developers across a range of AI fluency levels to understand where the real friction lived. What we found: the problem wasn't technical capability, it was mental model. Developers didn't struggle with what the tool could do; they struggled with knowing when and why to reach for it. The product needed to be opinionated about its own use cases.

The product strategy work identified the specific workflows where CodeWords added most value and the moments in developer workflow where those benefits would be most legible. Interface design built on that strategy — creating an experience that surfaced the right capabilities at the right moments, rather than requiring developers to learn a new paradigm before they could get value.

The Outcome

The research and strategy gave the Agemo team a clear view of where their product fit in the developer workflow and how to communicate that clearly. The interface design gave them a foundation to build on — one that prioritized legibility and appropriate timing over feature comprehensiveness. Sometimes the best product is the one that knows when to get out of the way.
