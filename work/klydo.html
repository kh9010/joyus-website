Klydo

Building a high-growth fashion startup for the instant gratification era

Client Klydo
Location India
Stage Pre-Series A, High Growth
Services Product Strategy, Brand Story, GTM
0→150 Daily sales in 10 weeks from launch
30+ Minutes/week shopping (top cohort)
4 Founding team members at start

The Challenge

Klydo arrived as 4 people with a dream: capture the new 15-minute shopping channel for fashion in India. The market leader Slikk was a year ahead with massive name recognition and 1,000+ orders per day. Klydo needed to figure out everything — customer acquisition, fulfillment logistics, the experience layer, and the revenue model that would drive CAC:LTV ratios strong enough for a meaningful Series A raise.

Our Approach

Where most Indian consumer businesses differentiate on price, we helped Klydo go somewhere more interesting: desire. We created a product experience focused on aspiration rather than transaction, powered by gen-AI that shows clothing in lifestyle settings users actually want to inhabit. The inspiration was short-form video and influencer reels — native to the audience, built on the grammar they already understood.

When the branding person quit after 6 weeks, we jumped in: logo, visual identity, brand voice. When team narratives diverged, we brought in a best-selling writer to unify the story across market and product. We became the connective tissue the founding team needed — across disciplines, across functions, across the moments when startups most need someone who can see the whole picture.

The Outcome

Launched before Diwali (October 20), pivoted the experience through holiday sales, and hit 150 daily sales before January 1. By working across product strategy, brand design, content systems, and GTM simultaneously, we helped the Klydo team see the forest while dealing with the many trees. That cross-functional view is what early-stage companies often need most.
