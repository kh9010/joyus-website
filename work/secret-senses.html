Challenge

Secret Senses, a hair care brand, needed an ethos for their design and packaging that would feel both clean and fresh while evoking nature with sensuality, grounded in Indian origins. The brand needed to stand out on shelf while maintaining an approachable, authentic presence that honored both the product quality and cultural heritage.

Approach

Built from natural shapes and flourishes, using line work that nods to Indian motifs, the identity system started with hand-drawn exploration. Divya hand-drew watercolor elements — flowers, leaves, fruits — which were scanned and vectorized to form the heart of the brand. A paisley-inspired S mark in teal (#55c2b4) became the logo, establishing instant visual recognition.

The intense saturated color palette balanced the soft hand-drawn illustrations, creating feelings of freshness, sensuality, and Indian-ness. Each product's pattern was composed from illustrations chosen to nod to key ingredients, making the packaging tell the story of what's inside.

Outcome

A brand identity built entirely around hand-crafted illustration — from sketchbook to shelf. The unique floral patterns created instant shelf recognition while the handwritten font and nature-inspired elements established the emotional warmth the brand needed. The system proved flexible enough to extend across product lines while maintaining coherent brand voice, allowing Secret Senses to build a distinctive presence in a crowded personal care market.
