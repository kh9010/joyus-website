secret senses

Hair-care brand in search of an ethos for their design and packaging.

  • Identity Design
  • Illustrations
  • Packaging
India 1–5 employees Bootstrapped

rooted in nature & Indian motifs

secret senses Identity Design Illustrations Packaging

Secret Senses packaging and branding builds from natural shapes and flourishes, using linework to nod to Indian motifs. Grounding in nature gives an emotional resonance that allows for brand extendability — a key requirement when future markets are on the horizon.

↓ then the illustrations themselves

hand-drawn, then vectorized

secret senses Identity Design Illustrations Packaging

Balancing gentleness with energy requires multiple paradigms to achieve the different feelings. Here, the intensity of the colors balances the soft hand-drawn illustrations — giving feelings of freshness, sensuality and Indian-ness.

↓ now onto the bottle

the brand on shelf

secret senses Identity Design Illustrations Packaging

Packaging carries the same quiet intensity as the brand book — natural shapes and flourishes, linework nodding to Indian motifs, color that feels both clean and sensuous.

↓ and the pattern that holds it together

a pattern that holds

secret senses Identity Design Illustrations Packaging

With a unique pattern, the brand is reinforced without overwhelming. Negative space balances the repetition of the brand illustrations and colors.

Illustrations chosen for the pattern nod to the key ingredients of the products — flowers softly peek through, embodying the Secret Senses brand value of "nature as a foundation."
