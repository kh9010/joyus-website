Challenge

XTDB, formally CRUX and a JUXT-owned company, is a general purpose bi-temporal SQL & Datalog database. After choosing the new name, they needed refreshed visual branding that would incorporate temporal ideas and database concepts while maintaining feelings of flexibility, creativity, and openness. The rebrand needed to work across diverse audiences — from engineers to investors — while honoring the parent company's identity.

Approach

Early generative concepts explored themes of remembering, permanence, and reliability — from elephants to tree rings, neural clusters to origami. Through exploration and iteration, the origami direction emerged as particularly strong for its mathematical, human-made, and inspiring qualities.

The final 3D origami mark uses subtle shading with colored X elements in an hourglass fashion, capturing the temporal essence of the database while maintaining recognizability with JUXT's parent branding. The mark works across contexts — from small favicon to large print applications. We created comprehensive brand guidelines with 9 logo variants for different contexts: full color, monochrome, small size applications, colored backgrounds, and inverted versions. The color palette incorporates JUXT's brand while establishing XTDB's distinct identity.

Outcome

A complete rebrand that captures the essence of a temporal database through an origami metaphor — folding time and data into something elegant and knowable. The brand guidelines ensured consistency across all existing and future artifacts, from website to documentation, from conference materials to product interfaces. The nine logo variants made adoption easy across the organization, reducing friction in implementation while maintaining visual coherence. The rebrand positioned XTDB as a mature, thoughtful tool in a technical landscape.
