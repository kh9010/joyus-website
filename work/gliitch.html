Challenge

Gliitch, a Hong Kong-based digital services provider, needed a visual identity that would differentiate them in a competitive market while communicating their creative, forward-thinking approach. They required a brand system that felt contemporary and digital-native, yet maintained the sophistication necessary to appeal to enterprise clients across Asia-Pacific markets.

Approach

We began with extensive moodboarding sessions to explore visual territories that would capture Gliitch's energy and positioning. This exploratory phase was critical — moving beyond assumptions to discover authentic visual directions that resonated with the team's vision and culture.

From these insights, we developed a distinctive brand identity with a strong typographic foundation, carefully curated color palette, and a system of visual elements that could extend across diverse applications. The branding work extended into comprehensive media guides ensuring consistency across all touchpoints, from website and applications to presentation templates, business collateral, and social media.

Outcome

A cohesive brand system that positioned Gliitch as a creative force in the Asia-Pacific digital services space. The brand guidelines provided clear direction for internal teams and external partners, making the identity easy to maintain and extend. The visual system proved flexible enough to work across multiple contexts — from technical documentation to marketing collateral — while maintaining consistent brand voice. Gliitch gained a distinctive presence that reflected their innovative approach to digital problem-solving.
